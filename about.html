About Tiny Paper Café

Tiny Paper Café was founded by Jaspinder, a graphic designer with a background in events and barista work, who loves creating spaces where people can connect, slow down, and create something meaningful.

After moving from Norway to Switzerland, she wanted to create a place where everyone could relax, paint, and write, with no experience needed. Inspired by the joy of sending postcards to friends and family, Tiny Paper Café was born from the idea that a small piece of paper can carry big emotions, memories, and stories.

More than just a painting café, Tiny Paper Café is a place to enjoy the full experience: creating a postcard by hand, writing a thoughtful message, and sending a little surprise to someone’s mailbox.

The menu is kept small but made with love, featuring coffee, matcha, chai, refreshing iced drinks, and homemade Norwegian treats.
